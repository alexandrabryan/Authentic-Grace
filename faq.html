Shipping Policy

Authentic Grace ships orders placed in the United States within 5 to 7 business days from the date of order.

Shipping costs a flat rate of $5.00. Orders exceeding $150.00 ship for free.

Return Policy


Authentic Grace accepts returns on unworn, unwashed, and undamaged merchandise purchased online or in-store with all original tags for a refund or exchange up to 14 days from the date of purchase.

A return fee of $5.00 will be deducted from the total refund. Authentic Grace will refund the money to the original form of payment. Please allow up to two weeks for your refund to be processed.
